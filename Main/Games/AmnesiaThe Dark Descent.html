Description:
Amnesia: The Dark Descent thrusts players into a terrifying world where the line between reality and nightmare blurs. You awaken in a desolate castle with no memory of who you are or how you got there. As you explore the dark, foreboding environment, you must piece together your past while avoiding the monstrous horrors that lurk within the shadows.
The game emphasizes stealth and exploration over combat, challenging players to solve puzzles and evade enemies rather than confront them directly. Your only means of defense is to hide, stay quiet, and manage your sanity. The game's sanity mechanic is particularly notable; encountering frightening events or spending too much time in the dark will deteriorate your sanity, leading to terrifying hallucinations and attracting the attention of hostile entities.
