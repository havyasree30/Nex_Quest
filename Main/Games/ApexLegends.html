Description:
Apex Legends is a free-to-play battle royale game set in the Titanfall universe, developed by Respawn Entertainment. It features a fast-paced, squad-based gameplay where teams of three players drop into an ever-shrinking arena to compete against other squads. Each player selects from a roster of unique "Legends," each with their own abilities and playstyles, adding tactical depth and variety to matches. The game's map evolves over seasons, introducing new gameplay mechanics and narrative developments.
