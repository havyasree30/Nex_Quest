Description:
Call of Duty: Warzone is a free-to-play battle royale game part of the Call of Duty franchise, developed by Infinity Ward and Raven Software. It features up to 150 players dropping onto a large map to scavenge for weapons, equipment, and resources while eliminating opponents to be the last team (or player) standing. Warzone integrates elements from previous Call of Duty games with its own unique mechanics, including the Gulag respawn system where defeated players can fight for a chance to return to the match.
