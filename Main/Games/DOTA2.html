Description:
Dota 2 is a free-to-play multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed and published by Valve Corporation. It is the successor to the original Defense of the Ancients (DotA) mod for Warcraft III: Reign of Chaos and its expansion, The Frozen Throne. In Dota 2, two teams of five players each compete to destroy each other's ancient building while defending their own on a map consisting of three lanes and a jungle. Players control "heroes," each with unique abilities and roles, and gain experience and gold to purchase items that enhance their abilities and strategy.
