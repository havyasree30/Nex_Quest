Description:
Dead by Daylight plunges players into a macabre world of horror and survival, where the only goal is to escape from a gruesome fate. As a Killer, you play in first-person, stalking and capturing Survivors using a range of horrifying abilities and deadly traps. As a Survivor, you play in third-person, working together with other players to repair generators and open gates while evading the Killer's relentless pursuit.
The game features a rich variety of original and licensed content, bringing iconic horror characters into the fray. From legendary movie slashers to terrifying original creations, each Killer has a unique set of powers that shape the game's dynamic and keep the gameplay fresh and exciting.
