Description:
Fortnite is a free-to-play battle royale game developed and published by Epic Games. It features cartoonish graphics and combines construction and shooting mechanics with strategic gameplay elements. Players can drop onto an island with up to 100 players, where they scavenge for weapons and resources to defend themselves against other players and the enclosing storm. Fortnite is known for its frequent updates, limited-time game modes, and collaborations with popular franchises such as Marvel and Star Wars.
