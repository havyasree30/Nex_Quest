PUBG: Battlegrounds (PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds) is a multiplayer battle royale game developed and published by PUBG Corporation. It pioneered the battle royale genre, where up to 100 players parachute onto an island, scavenge for weapons and equipment, and fight to be the last player (or team) standing as a shrinking safe zone forces players into closer proximity. The game offers a realistic shooting experience with a focus on strategic gameplay, positioning, and survival tactics.
