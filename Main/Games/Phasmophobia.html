Description:
Phasmophobia immerses players in the eerie and unsettling world of ghost hunting. As part of a team of up to four players, you will enter a variety of haunted locations, including houses, schools, prisons, and asylums, each with its own unique layout and ghostly inhabitants. Your goal is to use an array of ghost-hunting equipment to gather evidence and determine the type of ghost haunting the location.
The game features realistic graphics, ambient sounds, and a variety of supernatural entities, each with unique behaviors and characteristics. Communication is key, as players must work together to strategize, share findings, and complete objectives.
