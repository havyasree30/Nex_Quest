Description:
Project CARS Returns With Its New Sequel Known As Project Cars 2 PC Game. Slightly Mad Studios (A British Video Game Company ) Have Developed And Published This Racing Game Under Their Own Banner. 2016 Is The Release Year For This Simulation And High Speed Racing Game. This Game Comes With New Features Which Are Not Found In The Previous Game Project CARS. There Are 50 Different Locations With More Than 200 Race Courses. Normal Racing Tracks Along With Off Road Tracks Like Dirt, Gravel, Mud And Snow Are Also Included. Gamer Has To Race On All The Tracks In Order To Win The Races In The Top Position. He Has To Compete With Other Racers On Highly Detailed Race Courses Using High Speed Racing Cars. Various Weather Effects Along With Day And Night Time Periods Are Also Available.
