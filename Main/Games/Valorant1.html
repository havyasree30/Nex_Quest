Description:
Valorant is a free-to-play tactical first-person shooter developed and published by Riot Games. It blends precise gunplay with unique agent abilities, focusing on tactical team-based gameplay. Players choose from a roster of agents, each with distinct abilities that complement different playstyles and strategies. Matches are played in rounds where one team defends bomb sites while the other attempts to plant a bomb or eliminate the defenders. Valorant emphasizes strategy, communication, and precise aiming skills.
