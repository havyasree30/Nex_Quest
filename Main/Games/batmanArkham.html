Batman: Arkham Asylum

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Release Date: 2009
Devoloper: Rocksteady Studios
Publisher: Eidos Interactive
Type of Publication: RePack
Interface Language: Russian/English




System Requirements:

OS: Windows® XP/Vista
CPU: Intel 3 Ghz or equivalent AMD
RAM: 2 GB
GPU: NVIDIA 6600, ATI 1300 or better
Hard disk space: 6.5 GB




Description:
In Batman: Arkham Asylum, players step into the role of the legendary Dark Knight himself, navigating the dark and treacherous halls of Arkham Asylum. The game kicks off with the Joker's cunning scheme to take over the asylum, thrusting Batman into a high-stakes battle against some of Gotham's most notorious villains. As players explore the sprawling island, they'll encounter familiar faces like Harley Quinn, Scarecrow, and Poison Ivy, each with their own sinister agendas to thwart.
The game's captivating atmosphere immerses players in the gritty and foreboding world of Gotham City, with crumbling architecture, dimly lit corridors, and a palpable sense of danger around every corner. With fluid combat mechanics, players can unleash a variety of devastating attacks and counter moves, channeling Batman's unparalleled combat skills to take down waves of enemies. Alongside combat, players will need to utilize Batman's detective abilities to solve puzzles and unravel the Joker's twisted plot
Beyond its gripping main storyline, Batman: Arkham Asylum offers a wealth of side missions and collectibles to discover, ensuring there's always something new to uncover in the dark recesses of the asylum. With its stunning visuals, immersive gameplay, and faithful representation of the Batman universe, Arkham Asylum delivers an unforgettable gaming experience that will keep players on the edge of their seats until the very end.
