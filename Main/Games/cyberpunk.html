Description:
Cyberpunk 2077 is a large-scale game project developed in the genre of adventure action-RPG, the style of which is based on cyberpunk. From the very first minutes, the player is invited to plunge into the gloomy atmosphere of an alternative future, where he will face a considerable number of dangers and complete a number of important missions.
The plot line will tell us about the events taking place in 2077, where the future turned out to be far from what we imagine it to be. An important change is that people finally stopped believing in something natural and completely entrusted their lives to technology. Everything around has changed, and large-scale corporations have risen in power, setting their own rules and laws. The player will take on the role of the main character, who is an inhabitant of the city of the future called "Night City", named after its founder. The streets of Night City are filled with gray paint, multi-level buildings keep a lot of secrets, people are scared and live in ignorance, and cybernetic prostheses have become a main part of their lives - this is exactly the local atmosphere. The main feature of the gameplay is not only maximum realism, but also an accurate study of the improvement system, which will allow you to modify the character in every possible way, making him more powerful and bolder throughout the passage. Explore the environment, get valuable resources, gain new powers, destroy opponents and do everything possible to succeed.
