Description:
Halo Infinite is a first-person shooter developed by 343 Industries and published by Xbox Game Studios. It continues the iconic Halo series, featuring Master Chief as the protagonist in a vast, open-world environment known as Zeta Halo. Players engage in classic Halo combat against the Banished, exploring a dynamic landscape and utilizing new weapons and equipment, including the grapple shot for enhanced mobility. The game offers a campaign mode, multiplayer experience, and Forge mode for community-created content.
